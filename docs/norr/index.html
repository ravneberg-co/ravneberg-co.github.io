What is Norr?

Norr is a communication protocol designed for resilient, decentralized messaging. It works across any transport layer - from the internet to radio waves - and uses post-quantum encryption to ensure your messages stay private, even against future threats.

Key Features

Transport Agnostic

Works over TCP/IP, LoRa, packet radio, or any other transport. Your communication isn't tied to infrastructure that might fail.

Post-Quantum Encryption

Uses modern cryptographic algorithms designed to withstand attacks from quantum computers.

Decentralized

No central servers required. Messages can hop through any available node to reach their destination.

Low Bandwidth

Optimized for constrained environments. Send messages even over the slowest connections.

Status

Norr is currently in active development. The protocol specification is being refined and reference implementations are being built.
